Transform a video embed code into an HTML5 video tag that you can embed in an email. Play around with the example to see how it works, then clear it and paste in your own embed code! Just be aware that due to the limitations of email clients, videos embedded directly in emails won't be able to make use of Wistia's fancy functionality like Customize features and analytics.
