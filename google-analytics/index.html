Google Analytics

This lab has been deprecated. All Wistia embeds publish video metrics to Google Analytics without requiring this script. Learn more.

Push data on your website visitors' video viewing behavior to Google Analytics and be able view it in the context of your general web analytics. Play around with the example to see how it works, then clear it and paste in your own embed code! Just paste in a Wistia embed code and start customizing, or show me an example.

One video or all of them?

The Google Analytics plugin can target a single embed code of your choosing, or all Wistia videos on a page.
