Golden Ticket

Link all of your videos using Turnstile email capture so that if a viewer enters their email address for one video, they won't be prompted to enter an email address on any other videos.

Instructions

Install the script to the right in the <HEAD> of all your website pages. Then, when a user enters their email address into a Turnstile gate, they will not be presented with an email gate on any other Turnstile enabled videos on your website.
